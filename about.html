About Denvan Hair Braiding

We are a professional hair braiding service based in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. Whether you visit us at our salon in Frisco or we come to you — we deliver beautiful, expertly crafted braids every time. All services are by appointment only.

We specialize in all types of braiding styles — from classic cornrows and box braids to trending boho braids and knotless styles. Every client receives personalized attention and quality work.

Choose to visit our salon at Suite Salon in Frisco, or enjoy the convenience of our mobile service anywhere in DFW. Either way, you'll get the same expert braiding experience.
