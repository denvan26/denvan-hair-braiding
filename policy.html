Our Policies

Please read and understand our policies before booking your appointment. By booking, you agree to the following terms.

💰

Deposit & Payment Policy

  • A $50 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment.
  • Your appointment is not confirmed until the deposit has been received.
  • The deposit goes toward your total service cost.
  • Remaining balance is due at the time of your appointment.
  • Accepted payment methods: Zelle (preferred), Cash App (processing fee applies), and Cash.
  • Deposits are non-refundable and non-transferable unless Denvan Hair Braiding cancels or reschedules the appointment.
📅

Booking & Confirmation Policy

  • Appointments are booked on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Appointments are confirmed only by phone, text message, or email after the deposit is received.
  • Clients must send a photo of the desired hairstyle before the appointment so we can prepare accordingly.
  • A confirmation message will be sent 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.
  • If you do not respond to the confirmation, your appointment may be released.
⚠

Cancellation & No-Show Policy

  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.
  • If you cancel within 24 hours, your deposit is forfeited (non-refundable).
  • No-shows will result in a full loss of the deposit and may be required to pay a higher deposit for future bookings.
  • If you need to reschedule, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. One free reschedule is allowed per booking.
  • Repeated cancellations or no-shows may result in being unable to book future appointments.
⏰

Late Arrival Policy

  • Please be ready at your scheduled time. We travel to your location and plan our day around your appointment.
  • A 20 to 30-minute grace period is allowed. After 30 minutes, the appointment may be shortened or rescheduled.
  • If you are running late, please text or call us immediately so we can adjust if possible.
  • If the braider arrives and the client is not ready or available, a $25 waiting fee may apply after 30 minutes.
📍

Salon Visit Policy

  • Our salon is located at Suite Salon, 11990 Coit Rd Suite 2, Frisco, TX 75035. All salon visits are by appointment only — no walk-ins.
  • Please arrive on time or a few minutes early so we can start promptly.
  • Parking is available on-site. Please park in designated areas only.
  • Children who are not being serviced must be supervised by another adult at all times. Unsupervised children may not remain in the salon for safety reasons.
  • You are welcome to bring light snacks and drinks, but please keep the workspace clean.
  • Personal belongings are your responsibility. Denvan Hair Braiding is not liable for lost or stolen items.
  • Please limit the number of guests you bring to the salon to keep the space comfortable for everyone.
  • We maintain a professional and respectful environment. Loud or disruptive behavior may result in being asked to leave.
🚗

Mobile Service Policy

  • We offer mobile braiding service anywhere in the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. All services are by appointment only.
  • Please provide a clean, well-lit workspace with a comfortable chair and access to an electrical outlet.
  • A travel fee may apply for locations outside the standard DFW service area.
  • For safety, we reserve the right to decline service if the environment is deemed unsafe or unsuitable.
  • Please ensure pets are secured in another room during the appointment.
  • We ask that distractions are minimized so we can deliver the best results in a timely manner.
💇

Hair Supply Policy

  • Clients are responsible for purchasing their own braiding hair before the appointment.
  • We will advise you on the type, color, and number of packs needed based on your chosen style.
  • If you need us to provide the hair, it can be arranged at an additional cost. Please let us know in advance.
  • Your natural hair must be clean, detangled, and blow-dried or stretched before the appointment for the best results.
  • If your hair is not prepped, additional time may be needed, which could affect pricing.
💳

Pricing Policy

  • All braiding styles start at $200+.
  • Final pricing depends on braid size (micro, small, medium, large, jumbo) and desired length (bob, shoulder, mid-back, waist, butt, knee).
  • Prices listed on the website are estimates. Final pricing is confirmed after consultation.
  • Additional charges may apply for extra-thick hair, extra-long styles, added designs, beads, or accessories.
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice.
🛡

Health & Hygiene Policy

  • If you have any scalp conditions, allergies, or sensitivities, please inform us before the appointment.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service if signs of lice, open sores, contagious scalp conditions, or infections are present. This is for the safety of both the client and the braider.
  • All tools and supplies are sanitized between clients.
  • If you or anyone in the household is feeling ill, please reschedule your appointment at no penalty.
👶

Kids Braiding Policy

  • Kids braiding is available for children ages 5 and up.
  • A parent or guardian must be present during the entire appointment.
  • The child must be able to sit still for the duration of the service. If the child is unable to sit, the appointment may be stopped and rescheduled.
  • We use gentle braiding techniques designed for children's tender scalps.
  • Please have the child's hair washed, conditioned, and detangled before the appointment.
✅

Satisfaction & Redo Policy

  • Your satisfaction is our priority. Please inspect your braids before the braider leaves and let us know if any adjustments are needed.
  • Minor touch-ups can be done on the spot at no extra charge.
  • Redo requests must be made within 48 hours of the appointment with photo evidence.
  • Redos cover braiding issues only (e.g., loose braids, uneven parts) — not style changes or buyer's remorse.
  • No refunds are provided after the service has been completed.
📸

Photos & Social Media Policy

  • We may take photos or videos of completed styles for our portfolio and social media pages.
  • Your face will not be shown without your permission.
  • If you do not wish to have photos taken, please let us know before the appointment begins.
  • We love when clients share their braids! Feel free to tag us on social media.
🚨

Safety & Firearms Policy

  • Firearms and weapons of any kind are strictly prohibited in the presence of the braider during the appointment.
  • All visible firearms must be stored and secured away before the braider enters the home or workspace.
  • If firearms or weapons are visible or present during the appointment, the braider reserves the right to immediately stop the service and leave. The deposit will not be refunded.
  • This policy exists to ensure the safety and comfort of our braider who is entering your personal space as a professional.
  • Any form of threats, intimidation, harassment, or aggressive behavior toward the braider will result in immediate termination of the appointment, loss of deposit, and a permanent ban from booking.
  • The braider has the right to feel safe at all times. We ask that clients create a welcoming and respectful environment.
  • If the braider feels unsafe for any reason, they may leave without completing the service. No refunds will be issued.
  • Denvan Hair Braiding reserves the right to refuse service to anyone who does not comply with this safety policy.
🛡

Braider Safety & Respect Policy

  • Our braider is a professional providing a service in your home. Please treat them with the same respect you would expect in return.
  • Inappropriate behavior, comments, or advances of any kind will not be tolerated and will result in immediate termination of service.
  • The braider may bring a companion for safety purposes to any appointment.
  • The braider's location is shared in real-time with a trusted contact for every mobile appointment.
  • Please ensure the workspace is safe and accessible — well-lit, clean, and free of hazards.
  • Clients under the influence of drugs or alcohol will not be serviced. The braider will leave and the deposit will be forfeited.
  • We reserve the right to blacklist clients who violate any safety policies, with no future bookings allowed.

📝 Agreement

By booking an appointment with Denvan Hair Braiding, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to all of the policies listed above. These policies are in place to ensure the best experience for both clients and our team. Thank you for choosing Denvan Hair Braiding!
