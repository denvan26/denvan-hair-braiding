How Our Pricing Works

All braiding styles start at $200+. Your final price depends on two factors:

📏 Braid Size

Smaller braids require more time and precision, increasing the price.

Jumbo → Large → Medium → Small → Micro

$ Lower → $$$ Higher

📐 Braid Length

Longer styles require more hair and time, increasing the price.

Bob → Shoulder → Mid-back → Waist → Butt → Knee

$ Lower → $$$ Higher
